Beyond the Spotlight:

And when she needs a break from the spotlight, she dives into a good romance novel (current obsession: "More Than Lust" by [insert name]). From Kabwe to the world, watch out, fashion world, Namasiku's coming for your throne (and maybe your runway lights). Personal Insights (with a Dash of Vulnerability): Stage fright?
