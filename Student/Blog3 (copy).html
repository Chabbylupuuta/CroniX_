Why the Mafishi? Let's talk fish puns and practicality:

"Our main customer? The student population," says Praise, the team's resident cheerleader. "We wanted to make life easier, ditch the struggle buses, and cruise around campus in style (and silence!)." And cruise they did! The Mafishi, not only a looker with its aerodynamic curves, but also a tech marvel. Track its speed, battery, and even location? You bet! This electric eel was James Bond with fins.

Challenges? Buckle up, it's a bumpy ride:

"Money, money, money," Praise sighs. "People love the idea of an electric car, but funding? Not so much. So, we hustled. Bake sales, car washes, you name it, we did it." Thankfully, Archu Innovation Hub swooped in like a superhero, offering a much-needed boost.

Another hurdle? Balancing school and Mafishi madness. "We barely slept, ate meger meals for days," Praise says with a grin. "But hey, dedication has its perks, like seeing your electric dream come to life!"
